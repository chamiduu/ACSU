What is SCIENTIA '21

Since its inauguration in 2001, through the diminutive history of a decade, the Science Union of Ananda College has carried out numerous projects for the benefit of students of our country, with the intention of developing the knowledge and the enthusiasm of the local students towards the stream of science and technology and paving their contributions towards the development of the motherland through science. Scientia, Ananda College Science Day has been the biggest & the most important the event among our projects.

We have managed it to hold some marvelous science days under various valuable themes in past years with the enthusiastic participation of students islandwide. Since 2001, Ananda College Science Union have expressed their passion for the field of Science & Technology organizing this mammoth event.

Carrying on this proud tradition, we as the 2021/2022 batch of ACSU have taken steps to hold Scientia '21 in a prestigious scale under the given health guide lines on 30th of June 2022.

The theme of Scientia '21 will be “Blending Science & Arts”.
